# Research Objectives

1. Establish a working taxonomy that differentiates AI tools from AI agents across a capability and autonomy spectrum.
2. Design a two-layer evaluation framework with universal and use-case-specific metrics.
3. Develop an enterprise-wide Agent Registry that captures identity, purpose, capabilities, permissions, and accountability context.
4. Integrate the framework with governance infrastructure and risk policies.
5. Prototype and validate the framework on at least two enterprise agent workflows.
6. Investigate organizational, technical, and regulatory factors affecting adoption.
