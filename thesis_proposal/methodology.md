# Methodology

This research adopts a Design Science Research approach.

## Phases
1. Exploratory Phase
   - Semi-structured interviews
   - Policy and document analysis

2. Design Phase
   - Framework co-design
   - Agent Registry schema development

3. Validation Phase
   - Sandbox prototype
   - Dummy workflow testing
   - Expert feedback sessions

## Validation Strategy
- Compare the proposed framework against baseline benchmarks.
- Assess governance coverage with and without the registry.
- Test simulated failure scenarios and traceability.
